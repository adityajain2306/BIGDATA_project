 Bank Customer Segmentation and Visualization Using Big Data Technologies
 
 This project, titled Bank Customer Segmentation and Visualization Using Big Data Technologies, aims to leverage
 big data tools and techniques to segment and visualize bank customers based on their transactional behaviors and
 demographic attributes. Utilizing a comprehensive dataset containing various customer details and transaction
 records, the project employs advanced data analytics and machine learning algorithms to identify distinct customer
 segments. Key attributes such as customer age, gender, location, account balance, and transaction amounts are
 analyzed to uncover patterns and insights. By implementing this project, banks can enhance their customer
 relationship management strategies, optimize marketing efforts, and improve customer satisfaction through
 personalized services. The use of big data technologies ensures the scalability and efficiency of the analysis, making
 it feasible to handle large volumes of transactional data
